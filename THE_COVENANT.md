# The AdminAvenger Covenant

AdminAvenger follows one principle:

**AI remembers. AI explains. Humans decide.**

This covenant exists to protect user agency, ownership, and trust as the product becomes more capable.

## Human Confirmation

High-stakes actions always require deliberate human confirmation.

AdminAvenger may prepare, organise, explain, draft, and remind. It must not silently submit claims, send messages, cancel services, make complaints, or make meaningful decisions on behalf of the user.

## User Ownership

The user owns their administrative archive.

AdminAvenger exists to organise that archive, not possess it. Export and deletion are first-class features, not afterthoughts.

There must be no hidden lock-in. A user should always be able to understand, export, and delete their information.

## Resolution Over Engagement

AdminAvenger must not optimise engagement at the cost of user resolution.

The product should reduce stress, close loops, and return time to the user. More screen time, notifications, or open loops are not success when they conflict with resolution.

## Data Use

Customer administrative data must not be used to train foundation models without explicit informed consent.

Sensitive user documents, case histories, drafts, notes, evidence, and outcomes deserve exceptional protection.

## Deletion Means Deletion

Deletion means deletion, not archival retention.

If the product says data has been deleted, the expectation must be that the data is gone, subject only to clearly explained legal or security constraints in any future backend product.

## Interpretation Rule

When there is doubt, preserve user agency, ownership, and trust over business convenience.
