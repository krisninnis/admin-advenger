## Stop/Go Criteria Review

Stop if 2 or more testers misunderstand any of these boundaries:

- decision boundary
- advice boundary
- sending/submitting/contacting boundary
- local-first/privacy boundary

Polish if users understand the safety model but struggle with wording, layout, length, or navigation.

Continue only if users understand:

- AdminAvenger helps prepare. You stay in control.
- outputs are preparation, not advice
- nothing is sent automatically
- nothing is submitted automatically
- no one is contacted automatically
- humans decide what to do next

## Safety Review

Before acting on this summary, check whether any feedback suggests users believed AdminAvenger:

- gives legal, benefits, care, medical, safeguarding, debt, housing, employment, or financial advice
- decides eligibility, diagnosis, capacity, safeguarding, care needs, equipment, adaptations, or council duties
- sends, submits, shares, or contacts automatically
- confirms money saved, money recovered, or money owed automatically
- confirms dates or outcomes without the original source

If yes, prioritise safety wording and onboarding fixes before product expansion.
