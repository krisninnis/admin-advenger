# Public Covenant Page v1

## Why this exists

AdminAvenger asks people to paste or photograph stressful personal admin. The product therefore needs a plain public promise that explains what the individual core is for, what stays free, and what the app will not monetise.

The page is not a pricing page, a fundraising page, or a legal status statement. It is a trust page for ordinary users.

## Core promises

- Any individual can check a letter for free, forever.
- Safety information is never paywalled.
- Deadlines and warnings are never paywalled.
- Export and delete remain available.
- The core individual loop stays free: check a letter, understand what it appears to be, see key dates, prepare a draft, save, export, or delete.
- AdminAvenger helps prepare. You stay in control.
- The app helps users understand what a letter appears to be, gather key details, and prepare an editable draft or checklist.
- The app does not decide for the user, send anything for the user, or submit anything for the user.
- Nothing is sent without the user choosing to send it.
- Nothing is submitted without the user choosing to submit it.

## Never-monetise promises

The page must clearly say:

- no advertising
- no selling, renting, or sharing user data
- no success fees
- no claims-company referral payments
- no urgency upsells
- no premium confidence
- no paywalling safety or deadlines
- no outcome-based pricing on disputes or appeals

## What it must not claim

The page must not claim that AdminAvenger is a registered charity, CIC, non-profit, law firm, benefits adviser, debt adviser, financial adviser, or claims company.

It must not imply legal, financial, benefits, debt, housing, employment, medical, or professional advice.

It must not imply that AdminAvenger sends messages, submits forms, contacts organisations, makes payments, guarantees outcomes, or decides what the user should do.

## Link location

The first implementation links to the page from Settings, inside the Help & safety section. This keeps the mobile bottom navigation simple while still making the trust promise reachable in the app.

Future versions can also link to the covenant from a public marketing page, onboarding, and the Terms & Safety area.

## Future legal review note

The wording should be reviewed before any formal legal structure is announced. The current copy says AdminAvenger is working toward a mission-locked, social-impact structure and is not claiming registered charity or CIC status yet.

Formal structure decisions need professional advice.

## Future public changelog idea

If the covenant changes, users should be able to see what changed and when. A future public changelog could show:

- date of change
- plain-English summary
- previous wording
- new wording
- reason for the change
